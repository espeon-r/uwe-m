Profile & Skills

A football analyst and journalist with global reach and an editorial eye.

Who I am

I'm a football analyst and journalist at Futbol Mundial, where I've spent the past four years producing match analysis, tactical breakdowns, and feature content for audiences around the world. I covered the 2023 FIFA Women's World Cup in Australia and New Zealand, and contributed to the 2022 FIFA World Cup coverage from Qatar.

My career has taken me from the press box at FIFA tournaments to a fellowship with the Football Association of Zambia in Lusaka, and a Digital Sports Operations internship with NBA Africa in Johannesburg. Each role has expanded how I think about communication, storytelling, and the power of connecting with an audience.

I'm currently completing a Master's degree in Sports Administration at Northwestern University, where I'm building the academic framework to complement what I've learned in the field. I hold citizenship in Canada, Uganda, and the United Kingdom, and I'm a U.S. permanent resident — a background that shapes my understanding of global football and its many different cultures.

I believe the best football analysis is the kind that a casual fan can follow and a coach can learn from. That's the standard I hold my work to.
